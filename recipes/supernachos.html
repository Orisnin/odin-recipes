Super Nachos

Description

Preparing nachos with a typical Mexican recipe is easier than you think and can be the perfect snack to liven up a party or a delicious starter for a meal at home.

This makes a huge meal-sized tray of nachos with lots of good stuff! You can adjust ingredient quantities to suit your preference. Serve with extra chips if required.
