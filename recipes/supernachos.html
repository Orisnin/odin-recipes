Super Nachos

Description

Preparing nachos with a typical Mexican recipe is easier than you think and can be the perfect snack to liven up a party or a delicious starter for a meal at home. This dish can be made with different types of sauces and with the original pico de gallo recipe.

This makes a huge meal-sized tray of nachos with lots of good stuff! You can adjust ingredient quantities to suit your preference. Serve with extra chips if required.

Ingredients

Servings: 12

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • Â¾ cup water
  • 1 cup tomate paste
  • 1 (18 ounce) package restaurant-style tortilla chips
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, or more to taste
  • 1 L (33,8 ounce) Cheddar cheese sauce
  • 1 (15.5 ounce) can refried black beans
  • 1 cup guacamole dip
  • 1 cup sour cream, or more to taste
  • 8 tomatos
  • 1 package cilantro
  • 4 green onions
  • 3 lemons
  • 1 (4 ounce) can sliced jalapeno peppers,drained

Steps

  1. Cook and stir ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until meat is crumbly and no longer pink, 5 to 10 minutes. Drain excess grease. Stir in taco seasoning mix, water and tomato paste; simmer until beef mixture has thickened, 20 to 30 minutes.
  2. prepare the pico de gallo: finely dice the tomatoes and onions, as well as the cilantro and jalapeno peppers, all in a bowl. Squeeze the lemons and add the lemon juice to the mixture. Mix all and reserve.
  3. Put the Cheddar cheese sauce to heat two minutes in the microwave.
  4. Spread tortilla chips on a platter; top tortilla chips with ground beef, Cheddar cheese sauce and shredded Cheddar cheese, guacamole dip, sour cream and pico de gallo, sprinkle with refried black beans.
